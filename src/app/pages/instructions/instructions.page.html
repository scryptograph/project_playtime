Instructions

Connect Tablet to SwitchSidekick

Power on the Switch board and make sure it is near the tablet. Make sure that both the Tablet and the Switch Board has sufficient charge to continue operation.

Connect to Bluetooth

Open Bluetooth Settings and Connect to "ESP32 HID Device" If you can't find this device in your list, then refresh and wait a few minutes.

Start Playing!

Once the Switch Board has connected, go to the Home page, click on Start Module and verify that the keys on the Board work in game.
